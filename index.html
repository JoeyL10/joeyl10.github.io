Trip to Dubai

This is my my wife and I visiting her sister in Dubai in front of the Burj Khalifa. The building behind us is the tallest building in the world. It was originally called the Burj Dubai but the bonds issued to fund it nearly went into default before being bailed out by the government of Abu Dhabi. It was then renamed to Burj Khalifa after the ruler of Abu Dhabi and president of the United Arab Emirates, Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan.
